About Us

Furnifit Inc. is at the forefront of revolutionizing the way people experience home decor and furniture shopping. As a technology company, we specialize in providing innovative solutions that seamlessly blend the digital and physical worlds. Our flagship product is a Home and Furniture application that integrates augmented reality (AR) to transform the way individuals envision and furnish their living spaces.

Mission

At FurniFit, our mission is clear: to revolutionize furniture shopping by empowering customers to make informed decisions with the aid of augmented reality technology. Our commitment to this mission begins with our state-of-the-art app, which allows users to virtually place furniture items in their living spaces, bridging the gap between imagination and reality. Beyond technology, we curate a diverse selection of high-quality furniture to cater to different tastes and needs, ensuring that our customers can confidently find the perfect match for their space.

Exceptional customer support is integral to our mission, as we strive to provide an outstanding user experience. Moreover, we are committed to promoting sustainability by offering eco-conscious furniture options and educating our customers about environmentally responsible choices. In our pursuit of excellence, we continuously innovate our app and aim to foster a community of furniture enthusiasts who share their experiences, ideas, and insights, creating a space where individuals can learn and grow together.

Vision

"To revolutionize the way people shop for furniture by providing a seamless and immersive experience, where customers can confidently visualize and test how furniture fits within their living spaces through our cutting-edge augmented reality app."
